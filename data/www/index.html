TouchOSC Setup Guide

1. Open TouchOSC on your device and tap the ▶ Play button to enter control mode.

2. Go to Settings → Connections → OSC and add a new connection:

  • Host: (loading...)
  • Send Port: 8000
  • Receive Port: 9000

3. In the TouchOSC Editor, add faders/buttons with these OSC addresses:

Address PatternTypeDescription
/dmxinterceptor/fixture/{id}/redFader 0-255Fixture red
/dmxinterceptor/fixture/{id}/greenFader 0-255Fixture green
/dmxinterceptor/fixture/{id}/blueFader 0-255Fixture blue
/dmxinterceptor/fixture/{id}/dimmerFader 0-255Fixture dimmer
/dmxinterceptor/group/{id}/redFader 0-255Group red
/dmxinterceptor/macro/{id}/triggerButtonTrigger macro
/dmxinterceptor/blackoutToggleMaster blackout
/dmxinterceptor/masterFader 0-255Master dimmer

Fixture IDs: See the Fixtures tab for the ID of each configured fixture.
